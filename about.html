About Sparkle & Co

Rooted in Lesotho. Built on passion. Driven by unforgettable moments.

Our Story

Sparkle & Co was born from a deep love for Lesotho and a belief that this beautiful mountain kingdom deserves world-class events. We started with a simple idea — that every celebration, retreat, and gathering should feel extraordinary. Today, we bring that same passion to every client, every venue, and every occasion.

From intimate weddings in mountain valleys to large corporate retreats, we have grown into one of Lesotho's most trusted event planning companies, proudly celebrating local culture at every step.

Our Vision

To be the most goal-oriented events planning business in Lesotho — designing seamless events that bring engagement amongst stakeholders and inspire the future generation.

Our Mission

To create remarkable experiences by providing exceptional entertainment and networking opportunities to all adults and music lovers across Lesotho and beyond.

Our Values

Passion

Culture
